iPhone 11 Pro Max Reviews

I bought the new iPhone 11 pro max a month ago and it has been beautifully. I am an amateur photograhpher and
when I cant use my professional camera the iphone 11 pro max gets the job done with its triple lens cam. The
batter life is amazing!!! I can wake up in the morning with a full charge, use it all day, go out all night and
when I wake up in the morning it will still be on!! I store a ton of photos and videos so the 512GB memory is
a necessity. All in all this is an amazing phone and I would reccommend it to anyone!!
